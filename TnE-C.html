Dr. Steve Brule

In general, he is not a fan of "hunks". He is, however, a fan of sweet-berry wine as evidenced by his appearance on the channel 5 news segment where he had a bit too much and made a pass at Jan. He remains full of all sorts of useful tips expanding vastly beyond your health, proving that the Dr is master of more than just medicine.
